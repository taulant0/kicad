Breadboard Power Supply

This project is a compact power supply that plugs directly into a breadboard and provides both 3.3V and 5V regulated outputs. It’s a practical tool for powering small electronic circuits and is ideal for anyone working with microcontrollers or sensors. Below, you can see the bill of materials, schematic, PCB layout, and 3D model of the project.
